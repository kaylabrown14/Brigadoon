Project Highlight: Brigadoon

How did I find Brigadoon?
It all started when I was looking around my basement for vinyls. I asked my mom where the record albums were and she told me to check the bookshelf. When I looked through the bookshelf, I found an album with people dancing in plaid kilts and bagpipes. It read "Brigadoon", not to be confused with the Apple TV series, "Schmigadoon". I knew I had to do this one because it intrigued me and who knows? It could be fun to redesign.
The Process
Designing Brigadoon was no easy feat. I went through iteration after iteration, while remaining vibrant yet traditional to the Scotland background. I used Adobe Photoshop and Illustrator to design the cover and the inside cover as well.

Lyric Video of "I'll Go Home with Bonnie Jean"

Brigadoon is a musical about two men, Tommy Albright and Jeff Douglass, from the hustlin bustlin city of New York who find themselves lost and finding a place to stay out of nowhere called Brigadoon. Brigadoon is a magical place in the Highlands of Scotland. It is filled with music, dance, and plenty of bagpipes. When Tommy falls in love with Fiona, he has a choice to make. Stay in Brigadoon with his one true love or go back to the Big Apple with his best friend Jeff?
